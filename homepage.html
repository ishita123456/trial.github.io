More from Creator's Garage

Introduction

Creator's Garage is an ever-evolving community of learners who always believe in exploring the incredible opportunities available in the world, with their skills , understandings and capabilities . Working on the motto TEAM - Together Everyone Achieves More, we identify the need to empower students with the project-based learning experience and, therefore , tirelessly accomplish it.

Our Mission

Our Club is dedicated to improvising IOT, Embedded systems, MATLAB projects, Robot Designing and modelling. To further formulate a learning procedure more efficient than ever, we likewise have four sub-teams - ROBOCORE and SOFTX, each functioning in their respective areas of specialisation.

Our Vision

Striving to create a community of like-minded individuals where everyone can act together and put up a step ahead to acheive their intents. Perpetually inclined to impart a project-based learning experience with real-hands-on training.

Features

  • Encouragement to develop creative projects to solve daily life problems.
  • To offer guidance and financial assistance to the community members.
  • Opportunities to express skills, knowledge and creativity through conceptualizing, designing and programming machines.
